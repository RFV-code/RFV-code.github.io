The recent wave of blockchain crowd-funding initiatives have quickly set the stage for the best investment opportunities in the last few years.

Here, I have created a simple solution to diversify in the Bitcoin and Ethereum startup space. The keys to low risk investing are research and experience. Due diligence, proper analysis of what's actually on offer, terms and conditions, and the actual legal structure of these initiative. Instead it seem that folks are willing to take bets on these because they view them as sound investments and because of past performance alone.

Send your ICOFund tokens back to 0xA813e13d85D61c36D2fEc4621Ce85B5DDE9e83DF.
We sell the corresponding amount of fund allocation tokens and stocks that make up the fund for Ether.
We send your Ether back to your Ethereum address.
